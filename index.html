SUPPORT US

To continue working for the women we require your valuable support. We have numerous programmes running in several states of South India. We run solely on donations and to sustain our projects and continue setting up more of them, your support is absolutely vital.

We take pride in the fact that in 2015, with the generous support of our donors, we were able to give huge number of women a better future.

Any amount donated by you will be utilized by Women Empowerment Trust to strategize and execute on-ground projects to benefit the most vulnerable Women of India. We ensure that our response is timely and is delivered at an appropriate scale. While we appreciate one-time donations, monthly donations help us plan our programmes better and sustain them for a longer duration.
