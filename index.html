Who we are

Women Empowerment Trust is Registered under Indian Trust Act in the year 2009 for the vision of promoting women empowerment through health, education and economic development. WE-Trust has plan to work in rural and slum population. Presently it has plan to work on Health specifically to the personal health of women through training and providing health education, preventive education, Curative programs, rehabilitation and sustainable health through low cost herbal medicine.

Similarly on education programs focus to girl children education, special coaching for students of slow learner and successful in career education.

Economy is the major problem of women hence we need to identify , conduct feasiblity study and set us successful income generation Projects.

These are main three areas WE trust is planning to do for the next five years

SUPPORT US

To continue working for the women we require your valuable support. We have numerous programmes running in several states of South India. We run solely on donations and to sustain our projects and continue setting up more of them, your support is absolutely vital.

We take pride in the fact that in 2015, with the generous support of our donors, we were able to give huge number of women a better future.

Any amount donated by you will be utilized by Women Empowerment Trust to strategize and execute on-ground projects to benefit the most vulnerable Women of India. We ensure that our response is timely and is delivered at an appropriate scale. While we appreciate one-time donations, monthly donations help us plan our programmes better and sustain them for a longer duration.

Please come forward and donate whatever amount you can afford. Even a nominal amount of ₹500 per month will make a big difference in the life of a Women in need. Your support can help us rescue a Women from moving to the dark side of life. It can empower us help a malnourished lead a healthy life.

Please follow us on Facebook and Twitter to get updates on our work.

Women Empowerment Trust is very transparent with its spends. Last year, we spent 89% of what we got as donations to run programmes across India.Click here to download our Annual Reports.

All donations made to Women Empowerment Trust are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.
